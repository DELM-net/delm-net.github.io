Databases of Early Latin Manuscripts (DELM)

DELM is a network of researchers and research projects that aims to facilitate data exchange between databases of early Latin manuscripts.

[…]

The network was founded in May 2023. Current co-ordinators are Immo Warntjes (Trinity College, Dublin) and Pádraic Moran (University of Galway).
